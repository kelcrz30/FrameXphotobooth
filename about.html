Privacy and Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Kel Studio! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we
handle your images and personal data while you use our photobooth application.

Information We Do Not Collect

We prioritize privacy, so we do not collect, store, or process any of your personal data or images.

Image Processing & Storage

  • All image processing occurs entirely on your device (client-side).
  • Your images are not uploaded to any servers and remain private.
  • Once you close the application, any generated images are not stored or retained by us.

No Data Sharing

  • We do not share your images or any information with third-party services.
  • There are no analytics, tracking, or advertisements within our photobooth app.

Security & Device Privacy

  • Since everything runs locally on your browser or device, no external access occurs.
  • Your device handles all processing, ensuring complete control over your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
Your continued use of Kel Studio signifies your acceptance of any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to contact us.
